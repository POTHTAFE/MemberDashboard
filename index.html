Frequently Asked Questions

Who manages the TAFE social media?
Our social media platforms are managed by Delaney Pape and Kendall Gaber, who serve as our Social Media Officers. They create engaging posts, share updates, and promote events to keep members informed and showcase our chapter’s achievements.
Who do I contact to see the finance spreadsheets?
Derrick Richard, our Treasurer, oversees all financial documentation including budgets, fundraising reports, and reimbursement records. If you need access or have questions, Derrick is the designated point of contact.
What are membership fees used for?
Membership fees support club operations. They help cover transportation to events, food for meetings, and supplies for projects, decorations, and promotional materials — all of which enhance the member experience.
Who manages this website?
This dashboard is designed and maintained by Derrick Richard, our Treasurer. With advanced web development skills, he ensures the site remains secure, functional, and up-to-date for all members.
Can I suggest new ideas or events?
Absolutely! We welcome member input. Share your ideas with any officer or submit them through our suggestion form. Many of our best events come from creative member contributions.
